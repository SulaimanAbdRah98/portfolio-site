About Me

I'm a technical artist with 7 years of industry experience and a fiery passion for everything art and tech. I started learning game development at 14 and have since shipped 3 games. I hold a WSQ Diploma/Associate Degree in Animation and Visual Effects from 3dsense Media School.

In my spare time, I enjoy mentoring others in game development and technical art. I also like studying films and games to develop my artistic sensibilities.

I often explore experimental projects to push my skills further. I'm always learning more about game art and tech, and I love applying that knowledge to my work.
